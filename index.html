😄About Me😄

Hi! My name is Aarushi. A Computer Science sophomore interested in Web Development, I was an Undergraduate Teaching Intern for the Freshman Seminar course (ECS 1100) in our university for Fall 2020. EPICS (which was an optional course I was hyped to take) really helped me understand the importance of teamwork in solving real world problems. I worked on designing a Beehive Monitoring System for UTDallas' apiary. To expand my knowledge and gain experience, I am looking for a Summer 2021 internship for Software Engineering or Computer Science.

I'm involved in SWE (Society of Women Engineers) as a Website Development Committee member. I love being an officer in SWE since it's helped me a lot personally and professionally. Apart from Computer Science, I love reading, listening to music, knitting, and learning Korean. (I've been a member of the Korean Language Club on campus since my first semester of college.)
